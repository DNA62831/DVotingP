About Doorsath Voting Pariyojana Web

The Doorsath Voting Pariyojana is a groundbreaking project aimed at transforming the electoral landscape through the development and implementation of an advanced e-voting system. This project addresses the inherent challenges and limitations of traditional voting methods by leveraging modern technologies to create a secure, transparent, and efficient voting platform.


It renders Simple and Accessible voter experience that eventually increases voter engagement and turnout. Auditable, Easy To Use, Secure and Reliable are key features of Doorsath Voting Pariyojana.


The primary objective of this project is to design and build an e-voting system that ensures the integrity of the electoral process while providing an intuitive and user-friendly experience for voters.


How it works

Set-up Election

On successful registration, with our effortless ballot setup, you can set-up election in no time. This process involves 3 steps:

ADD ELECTION DETAILS

Add election details like election date and time, candidate's names and photos.
ADD VOTER LIST

Drag and drop to import the list of voters from our convenient spreadsheet template.
REVIEW & LAUNCH

Once you've entered all the required details, review it and then you are good to go. Schedule a start/end date or immediately launch the election.

Vote

Anyone of age 18 or above, can easily vote through our e-voting website

Results

After the election ends, on approval of election officer, the result can be generated which is automatically tabulated. This is solely for security purposes. The outcome can be published and shared with the voters on your eVote website.
